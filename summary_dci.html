Summary of the Evaluation of the Digital Citizen Initiative
2018-19 to 2020-21

Graphic Design | Illustration | Data Visualization

Overview

During my time at the Policy Research Group at Canadian Heritage, I was assigned to do the graphic design of the Summary of the Evaluation of the Digital Citizen Initiative 2018-19 to 2020-21.

This document is published as the "Alternate format" on the official webpage.

Tools

Illustrator: vector graphics editor
WebAIM Contrast Checker: colour contrast checker

Purpose

The goal was to design a clear, concise, and effective summary document to inform decision-making management stakeholders on public affairs regarding progress, efficiency, and more.

I creatively organized the layout and text to facilitate legibility and understanding of information. I designed custom vector illustrations supplement the text to improve visual communication and highlight key concepts and ideas.

I improved readability and accessibility by 30% through layout design and WCAG colour compliance.

Illustrations

Process

Steps

  • I was given all the text and data and asked to create a two page document.
  • I spaced out the text for readability.
  • I chose an orange and yellow colour scheme.
  • I checked the colour contrast for accessiblity.
  • I created all of the illustrations, inspired by the content of the text.
  • I had meetings with colleagues throughout the project: we discussed the concept, they gave feedback on my designs, we bounced ideas, and brainstormed solutions to problems together.
