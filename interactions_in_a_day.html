Interactions in a Day

Graphic Design | Data Visualization

Overview

The goal of this project was to track data for a day and create a graph based off that data. I began by brainstorming, then I wrote everything I did the day before; after review, I thought creating a graph of my social interactions would be interesting. Our social lifes are dynamic. As social creatures, we communicate and connect with others frequently.
