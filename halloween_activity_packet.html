Halloween Activity Packet

Education | Illustration | Graphic Design

Overview & Purpose

This Halloween Activity Packet was created to provide elementary school children engaging and challenging Halloween-themed exercises to build skills and complete throughout the month of October, such as when they finish their work early, for homework, or to celebrate Halloween day.

The activities focus on various skills: language arts, arithmetic, visual arts, creativity, logic, hand-eye coordination, and object recognition.

Students can exercise their English language skills by building vocabulary with the Halloween-themed word search, practice their math skills by figuring out the missing parts of the candy patterns, and use their creativity to decorate their own pumpkin.

Pages include:

Word Search, Colouring Page, Crossword, Connect the Dots, Maze, Symmetrical Drawing, Finding Hidden Objects, Drawing, Counting Objects, Patterns
