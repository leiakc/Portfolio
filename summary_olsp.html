Summary of the Evaluation of the Official Languages Support Programs, 2003-04 to 2020-21

Graphic Design | Illustration | Data Visualization

Overview

During my time at the Policy Research Group at Canadian Heritage, I was assigned to do the graphic design of the Summary of the Evaluation of the Official Languages Support Programs, 2003-04 to 2020-21.

This document is published as the "Alternate format" on the official webpage.

Tools

Illustrator: vector graphics editor
WebAIM Contrast Checker: colour contrast checker

Process

Steps

  • I was given all the text and data and asked to create a two page document.
  • I spaced out the text for readability.
  • I chose a blue colour scheme.
  • I checked the colour contrast for accessiblity.
  • I created most of the illustrations, inspired by the content of the text.
  • I did various iterations of the graphs, modifying aspects based on the client's feedback.
  • I had meetings with colleagues throughout the project: we discussed the concept, they gave feedback on my designs, we bounced ideas, and brainstormed solutions to problems together.
